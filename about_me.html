About Me

Hi, I’m Burak Eroğlu, a Computer Science student and a passionate beginner photographer currently living in Budapest as an exchange student. I love capturing the intricate details of historic sites, statues, and beautiful architectural works, including the stunning Budapest Parliament.

Through photography, I aim to preserve the unique moments I encounter and the stories they tell. While my photos may have some technical and aesthetic imperfections, I strive to improve myself every day and push my creative boundaries.

Outside of photography, I enjoy playing games, watching series, and exploring new creative projects. This website is my way of sharing my journey and my favorite photos from my time in Budapest with you.
